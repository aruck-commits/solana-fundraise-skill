# Solana Grants Overview

A map of every major grant program in the Solana ecosystem — who they're for, how much they give, and when to apply.

---

## The three main programs

### 1. Solana Foundation Grants
- **Who it's for:** Technical builders — dev tooling, infra, open-source protocols, research
- **Range:** $5K – $100K+ USDC (milestone-based)
- **Stage:** Works at idea stage IF you have a strong technical background. Better with a prototype.
- **Turnaround:** 4–8 weeks from application to decision
- **Best for:** Public goods, tooling, infra that the whole ecosystem benefits from
- **Not for:** Consumer apps, tokens, meme projects, pure marketing plays

### 2. Superteam Grants
- **Who it's for:** Builders in Superteam regions (India, Nigeria, Vietnam, Turkey, Brazil, etc.)
- **Range:** $500 – $10K USDC typically, larger for regional initiatives
- **Stage:** Idea stage is fine — Superteam invests in people as much as products
- **Turnaround:** 1–3 weeks, much faster than Foundation
- **Best for:** Regional ecosystem projects, content, community tools, early-stage consumer apps
- **Not for:** Projects with no regional angle or community benefit

### 3. Colosseum
- **Who it's for:** Hackathon winners and accelerator cohort applicants
- **Range:** Hackathon prizes $1K–$100K+ / Accelerator: funding + network
- **Stage:** Need a working demo or at minimum a strong proof-of-concept
- **Turnaround:** Hackathon cycles run every few months
- **Best for:** Founders who want more than money — mentorship, investor intros, ecosystem credibility
- **Not for:** Solo projects with no team, non-technical solo founders

---

## Quick decision tree

```
Are you technical (can ship code)?
├── YES → Do you have a prototype?
│   ├── YES → Consider Solana Foundation + Colosseum
│   └── NO  → Start with Superteam, build toward Foundation
└── NO  → Are you in a Superteam region?
    ├── YES → Superteam grants are your primary path
    └── NO  → Colosseum hackathon (find a technical co-founder first)
```

---

## Stacking grants

You can apply to multiple programs simultaneously. Common winning stacks:

- **Superteam grant → Solana Foundation** (Superteam gives credibility for Foundation apps)
- **Colosseum hackathon win → Foundation grant** (hackathon win is strong social proof)
- **Foundation grant → Colosseum accelerator** (shows you can execute)

---

## What grant reviewers actually look for

1. **Real problem** — not "we're building on Solana because Solana is fast"
2. **Why you** — your background, why this team can execute this idea
3. **Ecosystem benefit** — how does this make Solana better for everyone
4. **Milestones** — concrete, verifiable, time-bound deliverables
5. **Traction** — anything: GitHub stars, waitlist, community members, prior bounties won
