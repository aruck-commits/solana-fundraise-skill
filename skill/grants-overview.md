# Solana Grants Overview

A map of every major grant program in the Solana ecosystem — who they're for, how much they give, and when to apply.

---

## The three main programs

### 1. Solana Foundation Grants
- **Who it's for:** Technical builders — dev tooling, infra, open-source protocols, research, education
- **Amount:** Milestone-based, tailored to each proposal
- **Stage:** Better with a prototype. Works at idea stage with strong technical background + public goods angle
- **Turnaround:** 4–8 weeks from application to decision, rolling deadlines
- **Apply at:** solana.org/grants-funding
- **Best for:** Public goods, tooling, infra that benefits the whole ecosystem
- **Not for:** Primarily commercial projects (ask about convertible grants instead)

### 2. Superteam Grants
- **Who it's for:** Builders in Superteam regions (India, Nigeria, Vietnam, Turkey, Brazil, UAE, and more)
- **Amount:** Meetup Fund up to $200 / Startup grants up to $10,000 USDC
- **Stage:** Idea stage is fine — Superteam invests in people as much as products
- **Turnaround:** 1–3 weeks, fastest of the three
- **Apply at:** earn.superteam.fun/grants
- **Best for:** Regional ecosystem projects, community tools, early-stage consumer apps
- **Not for:** Projects with no regional angle or community benefit

### 3. Colosseum
- **Who it's for:** Hackathon entrants competing for prizes and pre-seed funding
- **Amount:** $30K grand prize, $10K for top 20 (Frontier 2026) / $250K pre-seed via accelerator
- **Stage:** Need a working demo minimum
- **Turnaround:** Hackathon cycles run twice a year
- **Apply at:** colosseum.org/hackathon
- **Best for:** Founders who want more than money — mentorship, investor intros, credibility
- **Not for:** Solo non-technical founders with no demo

---

## Quick decision tree

```
Are you in a Superteam region?
├── YES → Apply to Superteam first (fastest, builds credibility for Foundation)
│         Then stack with Foundation once you have a prototype
└── NO  → Do you have a working demo?
          ├── YES → Foundation + Colosseum hackathon simultaneously
          └── NO  → Build something first, then apply to Foundation
```

---

## Stacking grants

Apply to multiple programs simultaneously. Common winning stacks:

- **Superteam → Foundation** (Superteam win is strong social proof for Foundation)
- **Colosseum hackathon → Foundation** (hackathon placement de-risks Foundation application)
- **Foundation → Colosseum accelerator** (Foundation grant shows you can deliver)

Load `agents/grant-stacking.md` to build your personal stacking strategy.

---

## What grant reviewers actually look for

1. **Real problem** — specific user, specific pain, specific number
2. **Why you** — proof you can execute (GitHub, Superteam history, prior projects)
3. **Ecosystem benefit** — how does this make Solana better
4. **Milestones** — concrete, verifiable, time-bound
5. **Traction** — anything: GitHub stars, waitlist, community members, bounty wins
