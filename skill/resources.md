## Ecosystem funding beyond grants

If grants don't fit your project, these are the next options:

- **Colosseum Ventures** — venture fund that backs top hackathon teams beyond the accelerator
- **Multicoin Capital** — most active Solana-focused VC
- **Placeholder VC** — Solana ecosystem investor
- **Anagram** — early-stage Solana focused
- **Asymmetric** — Solana native fund
- **Angel network** — Superteam connects builders with angels informally; be active in the community
