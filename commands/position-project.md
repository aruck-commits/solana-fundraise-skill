# /position-project

Find the strongest angle for your project before writing a grant application.

## What this command does

Takes your project idea and outputs the best positioning frame for each grant program — so you're not writing the same application three times and wondering why none of them land.

---

## How to run it

Type `/position-project` in Claude Code.

---

## The questions

**1. What are you building? (one sentence, no jargon)**

**2. Who specifically benefits from this?**
Not "Solana developers." Be precise: "Solana founders launching tokens on pump.fun" or "Superteam members in India who do content bounties."

**3. What's the one thing your project does that nothing else does?**
If you can't answer this, stop here — your positioning isn't ready.

**4. Are you in a Superteam region? Which one?**

**5. What changed recently that makes this the right time to build this?**
A new Solana primitive? A gap that appeared after a protocol launched? A wave of new builders hitting a problem you've seen?

**6. Who are the 2-3 most similar projects that exist?**
Name them. We'll use them to differentiate.

---

## Output format

```
Positioning Brief — [Project Name]

ONE-LINER:
"[Project name] is a [what it is] that helps [specific user] [specific action] 
so they can [outcome]."

FOR SUPERTEAM:
Angle: [Regional ecosystem builder / Community activation]
Frame: [2-3 sentences positioning for Superteam reviewers]
Key hook: [The one thing that makes this a Superteam grant, not a Foundation grant]

FOR SOLANA FOUNDATION:
Angle: [Public good / Infrastructure / Developer experience]
Frame: [2-3 sentences positioning for Foundation reviewers]
Key hook: [The ecosystem-wide benefit that justifies Foundation funding]

FOR COLOSSEUM:
Angle: [Product impact / Novel primitive / Market potential]
Frame: [2-3 sentences for a hackathon submission]
Key hook: [Why this is a startup, not just a tool]

COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE:
[Project X] — what they do, where you differ
[Project Y] — what they do, where you differ
Template: "[Your project] is different from [X] because [specific reason]"

RED FLAGS TO FIX BEFORE APPLYING:
- [Any positioning weakness the agent spotted]
```
